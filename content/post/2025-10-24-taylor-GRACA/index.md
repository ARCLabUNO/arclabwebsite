---
title: Taylor Receives GRACA Support!
date: 2025-10-23
summary: "Doctoral student, Taylor Gonzales, received UNO Graduate Research and Creative Activity (GRACA) funding."
---

Doctoral student [Taylor Gonzales](https://arcorrectionslab.org/author/taylor-gonzales/) received UNO Graduate Research and Creative Activity (GRACA) funding for her project, "Process evaluation of a prisoner transitional living program for returning citizens" under the supervision of Dr. Jenn Tostlebe. This funding serves as the foundation for her dissertation work.
