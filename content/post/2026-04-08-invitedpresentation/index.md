On April 8, 2026, [Dr. Jennifer Tostlebe](https://arcorrectionslab.org/author/jennifer-tostlebe/) and Dr. Jose Sanchez (TCU) delivered an invited presentation entitled “One of Many or One of a Kind? Gangs Across the Landscape of Criminal Groups” to the Department of Criminology and Criminal Justice at Texas A&M University–San Antonio.
