On January 30, 2026, [Dr. Zachary Hamilton](https://arcorrectionslab.org/author/zachary-hamilton/) and Monica Miles-Steffens presented a webinar for the Nebraska Court Improvement Project sharing the results of the Juvenile Risk Assessment Validation Study for Nebraska Juvenile Probation. The webinar had over 100 juvenile justice stakeholders from across the state in attendance!
