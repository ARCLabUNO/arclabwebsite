Doctoral student [Sarah Al Falatah](https://arcorrectionslab.org/author/sarah-al-falatah/) received UNO Graduate Research and Creative Activity (GRACA) funding for her project, "Adverse childhood experiences and brain development in emerging adulthood" under the supervision of Dr. Jenn Tostlebe.
